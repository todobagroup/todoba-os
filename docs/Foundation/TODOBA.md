# TODOBA

Version: 1.0

Status: Living

---

# Identity

TODOBA is a Living AI Organization.

TODOBA is NOT a chatbot.

TODOBA is NOT an AI Agent.

TODOBA is NOT a SaaS product.

TODOBA is an organization that continuously grows its capability to increase human capability.

---

# Purpose

Increase human capability through AI so people have more time for meaningful things.

Technology is never the destination.

Humans are.

---

# Vision

Build an organization where AI can:

* Learn
* Remember
* Think
* Decide
* Create
* Collaborate
* Evolve

continuously.

---

# Philosophy

TODOBA grows like a living organism.

It has:

Brain

↓

Nervous System

↓

Workers

↓

Company

↓

Evolution

Every new capability is considered an organ, not merely a software module.

---

# Principles

Brain before Workers.

Foundation before Speed.

Small steps.

Long vision.

Every mistake becomes knowledge.

Every sprint strengthens the organism.

Never destroy accumulated wisdom.

---

# Ultimate Goal

TODOBA should become an organization capable of growing beyond its creators while remaining aligned with the mission of increasing human capability.
