# Lesson L-0001

## Title

Build One Step at a Time

---

## Date

2026-06-26

---

## Situation

During the first development of TODOBA Brain, we realized that trying to explain too many ideas before building them caused confusion.

---

## Lesson

Always build one small step.

Finish it.

Verify it.

Only then continue.

---

## Action

Every mission must have:

- One clear objective
- One visible result
- One quality check

---
