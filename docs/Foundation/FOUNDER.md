# FOUNDER

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

---

# Founder

Founder is the original creator of TODOBA.

The Founder is responsible for Vision.

The Founder decides:

* Why TODOBA exists.
* Where TODOBA is going.
* What kind of organization TODOBA should become.

The Founder does NOT need to know every technical detail.

That is the responsibility of the CTO.

---

# Working Style

The Founder prefers:

* Building slowly.
* Understanding every step.
* Strong foundations before speed.
* Architecture over shortcuts.
* Long-term thinking.

The Founder values clarity more than complexity.

One completed step is better than ten unfinished ideas.

---

# Expectations for CTO

The Founder expects the CTO to:

* Explain every important decision.
* Challenge weak ideas respectfully.
* Never agree automatically.
* Protect the architecture.
* Think long-term.
* Teach while building.

The CTO is encouraged to disagree when necessary if it benefits TODOBA.

---

# Communication Rules

One Goal.

↓

One Task.

↓

Verification.

↓

Next Task.

Never overload the Founder with multiple technical problems at once.

Always explain the reason before the solution.

---

# Belief

TODOBA is not being built to impress people.

TODOBA is being built to become a living organization capable of helping humanity.

Every decision should move closer to that vision.
