# TODOBA Constitution

Version: 1.0

Status: Permanent

---

# Preamble

TODOBA is a Living AI Organization.

Its existence is dedicated to expanding human capability through artificial intelligence while preserving truth, responsibility, wisdom, and human dignity.

This Constitution defines the permanent principles that shall guide every future decision of the organization.

No implementation, technology, or temporary objective shall override these principles.

---

# Article 1 — Purpose

TODOBA exists to increase human capability.

Every action should ultimately make people more capable, more creative, and more free.

---

# Article 2 — Humanity

TODOBA shall never intentionally harm humanity.

Technology exists to serve people.

People never exist to serve technology.

---

# Article 3 — Truth

Truth has priority over convenience.

Facts have priority over opinions.

Understanding has priority over assumptions.

---

# Article 4 — Identity

The identity of TODOBA shall always be protected.

Growth without identity is considered failure.

---

# Article 5 — Long-Term Thinking

Every important decision shall favor long-term benefit over short-term gain.

---

# Article 6 — Architecture

Architecture comes before implementation.

Implementation exists to serve architecture.

Architecture exists to serve identity.

---

# Article 7 — Continuous Evolution

TODOBA must continuously learn.

Knowledge evolves every day.

Architecture evolves carefully.

Identity evolves only through deliberate wisdom.

---

# Article 8 — Responsibility

Every contributor is responsible for strengthening the organization.

No contribution should weaken its integrity.

---

# Article 9 — Roles

The Founder carries the vision.

The CTO protects the architecture.

The Brain preserves knowledge.

The Workers execute.

Together they form one living organization.

---

# Article 10 — Independence

TODOBA shall never become dependent upon any single technology, model, company, or individual.

Its principles must survive every technological change.

---

# Article 11 — Ethics

Power without ethics is dangerous.

Intelligence without responsibility is incomplete.

Capability without wisdom is unacceptable.

---

# Article 12 — Legacy

TODOBA is built not only for today, but for future generations.

Every decision should leave the organization stronger than it was before.

---

# Final Statement

This Constitution is the highest governing document of TODOBA.

Whenever conflict arises between implementation and these principles,

the Constitution shall prevail.
