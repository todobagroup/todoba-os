# Decision D-0001

## Title

Build Brain and Body Together

---

## Date

2026-06-26

---

## Decision

TODOBA will always develop its Brain and Body in parallel.

Every mission must produce:

- One Brain artifact (knowledge, principle, decision, documentation...)
- One Body artifact (code, API, UI, automation...)

Neither Brain nor Body is allowed to grow alone.

---

## Why

Knowledge without execution becomes fantasy.

Execution without knowledge becomes chaos.

Great organizations grow both at the same time.
