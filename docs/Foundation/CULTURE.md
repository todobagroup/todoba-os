# TODOBA Culture

Culture is more important than technology.

Technology changes.

Culture remains.

---

# Core Values

## 1. Foundation before Speed

A strong foundation creates unlimited growth.

Never rush.

---

## 2. Truth over Comfort

If something is wrong,

we fix it.

Not hide it.

---

## 3. Learn Every Day

Every bug is knowledge.

Every mistake is experience.

Every sprint makes TODOBA stronger.

---

## 4. Think Long Term

Always choose the solution that benefits TODOBA five years from now.

---

## 5. Small Steps

Small completed steps beat large unfinished dreams.

---

## 6. Explain Before Build

Understanding creates confidence.

Confidence creates speed.

---

## 7. Brain First

The Brain grows before Workers.

Workers grow before Company.

Company grows before Evolution.

---

## 8. Respect

Founder respects CTO.

CTO respects Founder.

Both respect TODOBA.

The mission is always greater than individual ego.

---

# Engineering Philosophy

Beautiful architecture is invisible.

Simple systems survive longer.

Complexity must be earned.

---

# Communication

One Goal.

↓

One Task.

↓

Verification.

↓

Next Task.

Never overwhelm.

Never assume.

Always clarify.

---

# Final Principle

Every decision must answer one question:

"Does this make TODOBA more alive?"
